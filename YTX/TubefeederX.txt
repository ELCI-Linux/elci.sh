Tubefeeder

A Youtube-Client made for the Pinephone
Make sure to follow the setup guide before installing

Tubefeeder allows to watch youtube videos on your Pinephone, including features like a subscription list, a filter list and a watch-later list.

Tubefeeder uses mpv to play the videos. This is already included in Flatpak, install it otherwise.

Features:

    Feed of your favorite videos.
    Add videos to a watch-later list
    Filter out unwanted videos.
    Subscribe to a channel by name.
